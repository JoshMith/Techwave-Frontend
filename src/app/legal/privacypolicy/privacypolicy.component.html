TechWave Electronics Kenya is registered as a Data Controller with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (Section 25, Kenya Data Protection Act 2019)

We process your personal data only where we have a lawful basis to do so. The legal bases we rely on are:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil your order, manage your account, process payments, and arrange delivery.
  • Legal Obligation: Retaining financial records for tax compliance; responding to lawful government requests.
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud detection, platform security, service improvement, and analytics — where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent: Marketing communications and non-essential analytics cookies — you may withdraw consent at any time.

3.5 Agent Referral Attribution

⚠ Agent Referral Tracking Disclosure

TechWave operates a Sales Agent Programme. When you access our website through a unique agent referral link, a session identifier is stored temporarily in your browser's session storage. This identifier is used to attribute your order to the relevant sales agent for commission purposes.

What this means for you:

  • If you arrive on techwaveelectronics.co.ke via an agent's referral link, your completed order may be attributed to that agent.
  • The attribution is session-based only — it does not persist across separate browsing sessions or after you close your browser.
  • Agents do not place orders on your behalf — you shop independently.
  • Agents receive only aggregate commission information and do not have access to your personal data beyond what is necessary for their role.

This disclosure is required under Section 25 of the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. By shopping via a referral link, you consent to this attribution.

5. Your Data Subject Rights (Section 26, Kenya Data Protection Act 2019)

You have the following rights in relation to your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@techwaveelectronics.co.ke. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

5.1 Right of Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

How to exercise: Log into your account and navigate to Profile Settings, or contact our DPO.

5.2 Right to Rectification

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

How to exercise: Update your details directly in your account settings, or contact us.

5.3 Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements (e.g., financial records we are required to keep for 7 years).

How to exercise: Contact our support team at support@techwaveelectronics.co.ke

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format (e.g., JSON or CSV).

5.5 Right to Object

You may object to processing of your personal data for marketing purposes or where we rely on legitimate interests as our legal basis.

5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on your consent (e.g., marketing emails, analytics cookies), you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

5.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC):

  • Website: odpc.go.ke
  • Email: info@odpc.go.ke
  • Address: Upper Hill, Nairobi, Kenya

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

6.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Password Hashing: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • Secure Connections: HTTPS/TLS enforced for all data transmission via Nginx with SSL certificates
  • JWT Authentication: Secure token-based authentication stored in HTTP-only cookies to prevent XSS attacks
  • Database Security: PostgreSQL with parameterised queries to prevent SQL injection
  • Session Management: Secure session handling with configurable timeouts
  • M-Pesa Data: We do not store M-PESA PINs or sensitive payment credentials beyond what is required for reconciliation

6.2 Access Controls

  • Role-based access control (admin, sales agent, customer) — each role has strictly scoped permissions
  • Sales agents cannot access customer personal data beyond their attributed order counts
  • Server access secured via SSH key authentication
  • Regular security reviews

6.3 Data Storage

  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Ubuntu VPS with Nginx and PM2)
  • Regular automated backups
  • TLS encryption in transit

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify the ODPC within 72 hours and inform you without undue delay, as required by Section 43 of the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (No Consent Required)

  • access_token: Authentication token (30-minute expiry)
  • refresh_token: Long-term authentication session (30-day expiry)
  • Session management cookies required for platform functionality

Functional Cookies (No Consent Required)

  • Shopping cart session persistence
  • User interface preferences

Agent Referral Session Storage (Disclosed — No Consent Required)

  • A session storage key is set when you arrive via a sales agent referral link
  • This key is session-only and is cleared when you close your browser tab
  • It is used solely to attribute your completed order to the referring agent

Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

  • Usage statistics and performance monitoring (only placed after your consent)

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • The cookie consent banner displayed on your first visit to our platform
  • Your browser settings (block or delete cookies at any time)
  • Opt-out preferences in your account settings

Note: Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using core platform features. Non-essential analytics cookies are only placed after you provide explicit consent.

8. Third-Party Services

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, profile information as authorised, and a Google account identifier. Google's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Payment processing is handled via Safaricom's Daraja API (STK Push). Safaricom collects transaction amounts, your M-PESA phone number, and transaction references. We do not store your M-PESA PIN. Please refer to Safaricom's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

8.3 Delivery Partners

Courier and logistics services receive your delivery address, contact information, and package details strictly for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Policy, or as required by law.

15. Glossary

  • Personal Data / Personal Information: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, use, and deletion
  • Data Controller: TechWave Electronics Kenya — the entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data
  • Data Processor: Third parties who process data on our behalf (e.g., cloud providers, email services)
  • ODPC: Office of the Data Protection Commissioner — Kenya's data protection regulatory authority
  • DPO: Data Protection Officer — our designated privacy compliance contact
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device by your browser
  • JWT: JSON Web Token — a secure, compact format used for user authentication
  • Session Storage: Temporary browser-side storage cleared when you close a browser tab
