Refund & Returns Policy

Effective Date: November 10, 2025
Last Updated: April 2026
Version: 2.0

At TechWave Electronics Kenya, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund & Returns Policy is governed by the Consumer Protection Act, 2012 of Kenya and explains your rights and our obligations when a return or refund is requested.

This Policy applies to all purchases made on techwaveelectronics.co.ke. By placing an order, you agree to the terms set out below.

1. Return Eligibility

You may return an item within 7 days of the confirmed delivery date, provided the following conditions are met:

1.1 Conditions for a Valid Return

  • The item is unused and in its original condition
  • All original packaging is intact (box, wrapping, foam inserts)
  • All accessories, manuals, and bundled items are included
  • Proof of purchase is provided (order confirmation email or order number)
  • The item has not been physically damaged, modified, or tampered with by the customer

1.2 Accepted Reasons for Return

  • The product is defective or faulty on arrival
  • The product received is significantly different from what was described or ordered
  • The product arrived damaged in transit (must be reported within 48 hours of delivery)
  • The wrong item was delivered

Important: If your item arrived damaged, please photograph the damage and the packaging immediately upon receipt and include these images when contacting us. Reports of transit damage made more than 48 hours after delivery may not be accepted.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless they are defective on arrival:

  • Opened software, digital product licences, or sealed items with broken seals
  • Consumable items (batteries, ink cartridges, disposable accessories) that have been used
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse, negligence, or accidental damage after delivery
  • Personalised, custom-configured, or special-order items
  • Items purchased during a final sale or clearly marked as non-returnable at the time of purchase
  • Items returned after the 7-day return window has expired

3. How to Initiate a Return

Follow these steps to request a return:

Step 1 — Contact Us

Email support@techwaveelectronics.co.ke or call +254 116 632881 within the 7-day return window. Include:

  • Your full name and order number
  • The item(s) you wish to return
  • The reason for the return
  • Photos of the item and packaging (required for damaged or wrong items)

Step 2 — Await Approval

Our team will review your request within 2 business days and confirm whether your return is approved. We may request additional information or photos.

Step 3 — Return the Item

Once approved, we will provide return instructions. Items must be securely packaged and returned in the condition described in Section 1. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item).

Step 4 — Inspection & Refund

Upon receiving the returned item, we will inspect it within 2 business days and process your refund if the return conditions are met.

4. Refund Methods and Timelines

4.1 Refund Method

All approved refunds are returned to the original M-PESA phone number used for payment. We do not issue refunds via cash or cheque.

4.2 Refund Timeline

  • M-PESA Reversals: Processed within 5–7 business days of return approval
  • You will receive an SMS and email confirmation once the refund has been initiated
  • Safaricom processes M-PESA reversals on their end — TechWave is not responsible for delays on Safaricom's side once the reversal has been submitted

4.3 Partial Refunds

A partial refund may be issued where:

  • The item is returned without all original accessories or packaging
  • There is evidence of use or minor customer-caused damage
  • Only part of a multi-item order is being returned

4.4 Delivery Fees

  • Original delivery fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product, or incorrect fulfilment)
  • Where TechWave is at fault, we will also cover the cost of return shipping

5. Order Cancellations

5.1 Cancellation Before Dispatch

You may cancel your order at any time before it has been dispatched for delivery. Contact us immediately via email or phone with your order number.

  • Full refunds for pre-dispatch cancellations will be processed within 3–5 business days

5.2 Cancellation After Dispatch

Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled. You may, however, refuse delivery and initiate a return under this Policy once the item is received.

5.3 Order Cancellation by TechWave

We reserve the right to cancel an order if:

  • The item is out of stock after payment
  • A pricing error was made
  • Payment could not be verified
  • The order violates our Terms of Service

In such cases, a full refund will be issued within 3–5 business days.

6. Warranty Claims

Some products sold by TechWave come with a manufacturer's warranty. Warranty terms are specified on the product page.

6.1 How to Make a Warranty Claim

  • Contact us at support@techwaveelectronics.co.ke with your order number and a description of the fault
  • We will advise whether the fault is covered under the manufacturer's warranty and assist with the claims process
  • Warranty claims do not fall under this 7-day returns window and are handled separately

6.2 What Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Physical damage caused by the customer (drops, liquid damage, etc.)
  • Faults caused by unauthorised repairs or modifications
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage caused by use contrary to the manufacturer's instructions

Ex-UK / Grade B Products: Pre-owned products sold as "Ex-UK" or "Grade B" carry a 30-day limited return window for manufacturing defects only, as they are pre-owned imports. They are not covered by standard manufacturer warranty unless explicitly stated on the product page.

7. Exchanges

We do not currently offer direct product exchanges. If you would like a different item, please:

  1. Return the original item under this Policy
  2. Place a new order for the item you want once the refund has been processed

8. Your Consumer Rights

This Policy does not limit your rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2012 of Kenya. You retain all statutory rights, including the right to:

  • Receive goods that are of acceptable quality and fit for purpose
  • Receive goods that match their description
  • A full refund for goods that are faulty, misdescribed, or not fit for purpose

If you believe your consumer rights have been violated and we are unable to resolve your complaint, you may escalate to the Competition Authority of Kenya (CAK) or seek legal recourse under Kenyan law.
