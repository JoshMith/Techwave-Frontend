This Cookie Policy explains how TechWave Electronics Kenya ("TechWave," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on techwaveelectronics.co.ke.

This Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy, which provides further detail on how we collect and use your personal information. By continuing to use our platform, you acknowledge this Cookie Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) by a website when you visit it. They allow the website to recognise your device on subsequent visits and remember certain information about your preferences or actions.

We also use session storage — a browser-based storage mechanism that holds data only for the duration of your browsing session and is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab. This is distinct from cookies but serves similar functional purposes in certain contexts.

2. How We Use Cookies

TechWave uses cookies and session storage for the following purposes:

2.1 To Keep You Logged In

When you sign in to your TechWave account, we use HTTP-only cookies to maintain your authenticated session securely.

2.2 To Enable Core Platform Features

Certain cookies are essential for the platform to function. Without them, features like the shopping cart and account access would not work.

2.3 To Attribute Agent Referrals

When you visit our website via a sales agent's referral link, we store a session identifier to attribute your order to the referring agent for commission purposes. This is disclosed in full in our Privacy Policy.

2.4 To Analyse Platform Performance

With your consent, we may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our platform so we can improve the experience. Analytics cookies are only placed after you provide explicit consent.

3. Cookies We Use

The table below lists all cookies and storage items currently used on techwaveelectronics.co.ke.

3.1 Essential Cookies — Required for Platform Function

These cookies are strictly necessary for the platform to operate. They cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality. No consent is required for essential cookies under Kenyan and general e-commerce practice.

access_token

Type: HTTP-only Cookie  |  Duration: 30 minutes

Purpose: Authenticates your identity for each API request. Stored as an HTTP-only cookie to prevent JavaScript access (XSS protection).

Set by: TechWave backend (techwaveelectronics.co.ke)

refresh_token

Type: HTTP-only Cookie  |  Duration: 30 days

Purpose: Allows your session to be renewed without requiring you to log in again. Automatically generates a new access token when the current one expires.

Set by: TechWave backend (techwaveelectronics.co.ke)

3.2 Functional Cookies & Session Storage — Enhancing Your Experience

These are used to remember your preferences and maintain your shopping session. No consent is required as they are necessary for the service you have requested.

Cart Session Data

Type: Browser Session Storage  |  Duration: Session only (cleared on tab close)

Purpose: Temporarily stores your shopping cart items so they are not lost during your browsing session before you log in.

Set by: TechWave frontend (Angular application)

3.3 Agent Referral Session Storage — Order Attribution

This is a disclosed, session-only tracking mechanism required for our Sales Agent Programme.

Agent Referral Identifier

Type: Browser Session Storage  |  Duration: Session only (cleared on tab close)

Purpose: When you arrive on our website via an agent's unique referral link, this identifier is stored to attribute any completed purchase to the referring agent for commission purposes. It does not track you across sessions or websites.

Set by: TechWave frontend when a referral link is detected

Legal basis: Legitimate interest and disclosed consent (see our Privacy Policy, Section 3.5)

3.4 Analytics Cookies — Platform Improvement (Consent Required)

These cookies are only placed after you provide explicit consent via our cookie consent banner. You may decline analytics cookies without any impact on your ability to use the platform.

Analytics / Performance Cookies

Type: Cookie  |  Duration: Up to 12 months

Purpose: Help us understand how visitors navigate and use the platform so we can improve performance, fix issues, and develop new features.

Set by: TechWave or third-party analytics providers (where enabled)

Consent: Required — only placed after you accept analytics cookies

3.5 Third-Party Cookies

When you use Google Sign-In (Google OAuth), Google may set cookies on your device as part of their authentication process. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other third-party advertising or tracking cookies at this time. If this changes, this Policy will be updated and your consent will be sought.

4. Consent and How We Obtain It

On your first visit to techwaveelectronics.co.ke, a cookie consent banner will be displayed. You will be given the choice to:

  • Accept all cookies — including analytics cookies
  • Accept essential cookies only — analytics cookies will not be placed

Essential cookies, functional session storage, and the agent referral session storage do not require consent as they are necessary for the service. Your consent choice is remembered for future visits.

5. How to Manage and Delete Cookies

5.1 Through Our Platform

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by accessing the cookie settings link in the footer of our website.

5.2 Through Your Browser

All modern browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. Below are links to cookie management guidance for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note: Disabling essential cookies (such as access_token and refresh_token) will prevent you from logging into your account and using the checkout. We recommend only disabling non-essential cookies.

5.3 Clearing Session Storage

Session storage is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab. You may also clear it manually through your browser's developer tools (F12 → Application → Session Storage).

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our technology, legal requirements, or business practices. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Display a notice on our website or in the cookie consent banner
  • Re-seek your consent where required for new non-essential cookies
